About LUCKYCROCHET

Handmade with love, stitch by stitch

Our Story

Founded in 2020, LUCKYCROCHET began as a small passion project during the pandemic. What started as a hobby quickly grew into a beloved small business dedicated to creating beautiful, handmade crochet items.

Each piece is carefully crafted with attention to detail and love. We believe in slow fashion, sustainability, and the beauty of handmade goods.

Our Mission

To bring joy through handmade crochet items while supporting sustainable practices and keeping the art of crochet alive for future generations.

Our Values

  • Quality: Every stitch matters
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly materials
  • Community: Supporting local artisans
  • Creativity: Unique designs for every style
