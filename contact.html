Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom order take?

Custom orders typically take 2-4 weeks depending on complexity and current workload. We'll provide a more accurate timeline during consultation.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Free shipping on orders over $75 within the US.

What is your return policy?

Handmade items can be returned within 14 days in original condition. Custom orders are final sale unless defective.

How do I care for my crochet item?

Hand wash in cold water with mild detergent, reshape and lay flat to dry. Avoid machine washing and drying.
